## WHO Blood Pressure Classification and Corresponding Color Codes

| Category               | Color      | Systolic (mmHg)    | Diastolic (mmHg)    |
|------------------------|------------|---------------------|-----------------------|
| Optimal                | Green      | < 120               | < 80                 |
| Normal                 | Green      | 120–129             | 80–84                |
| High-normal            | Yellow     | 130–139             | 85–89                |
| Hypertension Grade 1   | Orange     | 140–159             | 90–99                |
| Hypertension Grade 2   | Red        | 160–179             | 100–109              |
| Hypertension Grade 3   | Dark Red   | ≥ 180               | ≥ 110                |
